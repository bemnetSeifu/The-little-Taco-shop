Taco Trivia

Do you know how the origninal Taco was made?

Ancient Mexicans used freshly made, soft, flat corn tortillas and gave them with fillings like fish and cooked organs. It was a staple meal that provided vital nutrients and energy to those who consumed it. These tacos didn't contain the cheese, lettuce, sour cream, and tomato that we associate with the meal today.(Source: Twisted Taco)
